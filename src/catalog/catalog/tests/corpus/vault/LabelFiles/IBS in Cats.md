# IBS in Cats

Feeding a cat with IBS (Irritable Bowel Syndrome) or IBD (Inflammatory Bowel Disease) can be tricky, but many cats do achieve minimal or even zero symptoms with the right diet. While there is no one-size-fits-all food, here are the most effective approaches:

**1\. Novel Protein Diets**

Cats with IBD often react to common proteins like chicken, beef, or fish. A novel protein is one your cat has never eaten before, such as:

- Rabbit

- Venison

- Duck

- Pork

- Kangaroo (in some regions)
