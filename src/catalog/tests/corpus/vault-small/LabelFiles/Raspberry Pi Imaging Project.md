\## UPS
The X1206 is an innovative uninterruptible power supply (UPS) expansion board designed specifically for the Raspberry Pi 5B. It features a 4-cell 21700 battery holder, supporting up to 20,000mAh (with 4 21700 batteries). With its advanced features and robust power backup capabilities, the X1206 is perfect for even the most demanding Raspberry Pi setups.
