# Reily’s CAD Laptop

## Laptop specs for CAD

Requirements: handle the demands of current (2025) design and modeling software, and adapting to the future requirements of the next four years including potentially heavier system requirements due to AI models as part of design and modeling.

Here are some basic laptop configurations and specifications recommended for running CAD, graphic, and 3D applications that can be used as a guide to purchase your equipment:

## Minimum Hardware Specs

Operating System: Windows - MacOS or Linux are NOT supported.

CPU: Intel i7 or i9 (14th generation+, 16 core+) or AMD Ryzen 9 (8000+)

RAM: 16 GB+

Hard Drive: SSD, 1T +

Video Card Memory: 8 GB+

Video Card: Nvidia: GeForce RTX 4060+

Video Card: AMD: Radeon RX6700+ or 7600+

Graphics: Not shared or integrated. Not Intel graphics.

Display: 15"+ (minimum resolution: 1920 × 1080)

Interfaces: High throughput Ethernet

Interfaces: High throughput USB4 or Thunderbolt, for external hard drives or eGPUs
