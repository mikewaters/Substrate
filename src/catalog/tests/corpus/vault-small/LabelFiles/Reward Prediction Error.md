# Reward Prediction Error

**++[Dopamine Neurons](about:blank?type=ask-arc&q=Dopamine%20Neurons&unit_title=Dopamine%20Neurons&unit_subtitle=Most%20dopamine%20neurons%20in%20the%20midbrain%20of%20humans,%20monkeys,%20and%20rodents%20signal%20a%20reward%20prediction%20error.)++** — Most dopamine neurons in the ++[midbrain](about:blank?type=ask-arc&q=midbrain&unit_title=Dopamine%20Neurons&unit_subtitle=Most%20dopamine%20neurons%20in%20the%20midbrain%20of%20humans,%20monkeys,%20and%20rodents%20signal%20a%20reward%20prediction%20error.)++ of humans, monkeys, and rodents signal a reward prediction error.

**Activity Patterns** — These neurons are activated by more reward than predicted, remain at baseline for fully predicted rewards, and show depressed activity with less reward than predicted.
