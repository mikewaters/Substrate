### Tip/Sleeve

Two conductors, mono audio

### Tip/Ring/Sleeve

Three conductors, stereo audio, MIDI, or [balanced](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Balanced_audio) mono

### TRRS

Four conductors, for headset+microphone or video + stereo audio

### TRRRS

Five conductors.

## Circuitry options

Jacks can be open or closed (shunted) circuit in all or both of tip and ring. Shunted is used in older audio equipment, but not in modern electronics.
