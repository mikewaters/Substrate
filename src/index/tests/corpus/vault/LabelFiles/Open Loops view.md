# Open Loops view

ref: [The Open Loops (Problem).md](./The%20Open%20Loops%20\(Problem\).md)

Anything that is taking up residence in the brain because of its incompleteness is an open loop.

Includes:

1. “All the Things” that go on and on

2. Current well-defined and bounded Efforts (projects etc)
