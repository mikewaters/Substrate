# Treat your to-read pile like a river, not a bucket

Topic: [The Anxiety of Too Many Things.md](./The%20Anxiety%20of%20Too%20Many%20Things.md)

<https://www.oliverburkeman.com/river>

> I'm confronted on a daily basis,…, by "haystack-sized piles of needles." 

> treat your "to read" pile like a river (a stream that flows past you, and from which you pluck a few choice items, here and there) instead of a bucket (which demands that you empty it).
