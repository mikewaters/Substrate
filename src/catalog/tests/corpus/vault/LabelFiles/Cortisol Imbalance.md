# Cortisol Imbalance

source: [Claude3.5!Sonnet.md](./Claude3.5!Sonnet.md)

There are several ways to naturally increase cortisol levels in the morning for someone with an imbalance:

1. Light exposure: Get bright light exposure immediately upon waking, preferably natural sunlight or a light therapy lamp.

2. Exercise: Engage in light to moderate exercise shortly after waking.

3. Cold exposure: Take a brief cold shower or splash cold water on your face.

4. Protein-rich breakfast: Consume a breakfast high in protein within an hour of waking.

5. Stress management: Practice stress-reduction techniques like deep breathing or meditation.

6. Consistent sleep schedule: Maintain a regular sleep-wake cycle.

7. Hydration: Drink water upon waking to help stimulate cortisol production.

8. Limit evening caffeine: Avoid caffeine in the afternoon and evening to improve sleep quality.

9. Reduce blue light exposure at night: Use blue light filters on devices in the evening.

10. Consider supplements: Some supplements like licorice root or adaptogenic herbs may help, but consult a healthcare provider first.
